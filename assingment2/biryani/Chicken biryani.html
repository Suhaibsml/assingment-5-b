Chicken Biryani is a beloved and aromatic rice dish that tantalizes taste buds with its rich flavors and fragrant spices. In this culinary masterpiece, tender chicken pieces are marinated in a harmonious blend of yogurt, ginger, garlic, and an assortment of aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. The chicken is then cooked to perfection and layered with long-grain basmati rice, infused with saffron, mint, and fried onions. As the biryani simmers on a gentle flame, the flavors meld together, creating a symphony of taste and aroma.
