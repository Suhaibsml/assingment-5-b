Pista (Pistachio) ice cream offers a unique and distinctive flavor that captivates taste buds with its nutty and slightly sweet profile. Made with ground pistachios, it boasts a delightful pale green color and a delightful crunch from chopped pistachio pieces. Pista ice cream is a popular choice for those seeking a more exotic and gourmet ice cream experience, showcasing the delicate yet robust flavors of pistachios in every spoonful.
