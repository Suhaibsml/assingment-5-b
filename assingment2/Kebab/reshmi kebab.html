Reshmi Kebab is a melt-in-your-mouth delicacy that features succulent minced meat, typically chicken, marinated in a luscious mixture of yogurt, cream, and aromatic spices. The marinated meat is then skewered and grilled to perfection, resulting in kebabs that are incredibly tender and infused with a rich smoky flavor. Reshmi Kebabs are known for their silky texture and delectable taste, making them a popular choice for appetizers or as a part of a sumptuous meal.
